About The Team

We are a competitive club water polo team competing in the CWPA Atlantic Division. We practice regularly and travel across the region to compete against top collegiate club programs. Dedicated to excellence in and out of the pool, our team prides itself on sportsmanship, dedication, and building lasting camaraderie.
